Debt Management Strategies Project

Standard 13: Managing High Levels of Debt

45 Minutes Calculations Analysis

In this capstone project, you will analyze different debt management strategies through real-world scenarios, evaluate consolidation options, and develop comprehensive debt reduction plans. You'll demonstrate your ability to assess financial situations, calculate repayment options, and make informed recommendations.

Required Materials:

  • Calculator
  • Worksheet or digital document
  • Spreadsheet software (optional)

Project Tasks

Task 1: Debt Analysis (15 minutes)

Analyze credit card debt scenarios by:

  • Calculating minimum payments and interest charges
  • Determining principal reduction rates
  • Evaluating repayment timelines
  • Identifying potential debt traps

Task 2: Debt Consolidation (15 minutes)

Evaluate multiple debt scenarios by:

  • Comparing interest rates across different debts
  • Calculating total debt burden
  • Analyzing consolidation options
  • Determining potential savings

Task 3: Debt Management Plan (15 minutes)

Create a debt management strategy by:

  • Reviewing monthly income and expenses
  • Identifying available funds for debt payment
  • Prioritizing debt payments
  • Creating a realistic timeline for debt freedom
