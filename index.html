CURRICULUM OVERVIEW

COMPREHENSIVE FINANCIAL LITERACY SOLUTION

PFL Academy offers a complete Personal Financial Literacy curriculum designed to meet state educational mandates while providing an engaging, interactive learning experience for students. Our comprehensive approach addresses the challenges schools face in implementing financial education requirements.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized around 15 financial literacy standards, with multiple chapters per standard. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Content Delivery

  • Structured introduction to key financial concepts
  • Multimedia presentations and instructor-led discussions
  • Real-world examples and case studies
  • Interactive skill builder activities
  • Guided reflection and comprehension checks

Day 2: Learning Lab

  • Hands-on application of concepts through learning stations
  • Interactive digital simulations and tools
  • AI-powered assessment and feedback
  • Portfolio development activities
  • Collaborative problem-solving exercises
